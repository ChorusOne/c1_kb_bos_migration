# How to stake POL (Polygon)

## What is Polygon?

Polygon (POL) is a decentralized Ethereum scaling platform that aims to improve the scalability and usability of the Ethereum blockchain while maintaining its security and decentralization.&#x20;

It provides Layer 2 scaling solutions like sidechains, rollups, and plasma chains, enabling faster and cheaper transactions.&#x20;

Polygon also supports the creation and connection of multiple blockchain networks, enhancing interoperability among different chains. The POL token is used for governance, staking, and paying transaction fees within the Polygon ecosystem

## How to Stake

To start staking POL, first log in to [https://staking.polygon.technology/](https://staking.polygon.technology/) on the browser of your choice.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (49).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Please ensure that the browser has integrated any of the wallets supported by Polygon.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (50).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Then, click on Login and connect to the wallet of your choice.&#x20;

Click ‘View all’ to see all the wallets supported by Polygon. We have chosen MetaMask.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (51).png" alt="" width="357"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once you have connected your wallet, click on ‘**Become a Delegator**’, and search for ‘**Chorus One**’ amongst the list of available validators.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (52).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Chorus One validator seen above</p></figcaption></figure>

Click on ‘**Chorus One**’ to verify all the details. Ensure that the Validator address (shown as ‘**Owner**’) is:

* `0xbbd83024be631bb6f3dd3c0363b3d43b5d91c35f`

{% hint style="info" %}
Note: The commission rate to stake POL with Chorus One will be shown on the validator screen.
{% endhint %}

Once you have verified all the details, click ‘**Become a Delegator**’.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (53).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Next, enter the amount of POL you would like to stake. Then, click ‘**Continue**’.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (54).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You will be redirected to your wallet to approve the transaction, which can take up to a few minutes.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (55).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="success" %}
You have now completed the process and staked your POL with Chorus One! &#x20;
{% endhint %}
