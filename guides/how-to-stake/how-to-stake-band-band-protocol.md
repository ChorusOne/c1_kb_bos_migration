# How to stake BAND (Band Protocol)

## How to Stake BAND (Band Protocol)

Welcome to the step-by-step guide on how to stake BAND tokens on the Band Protocol using Chorus One. Staking BAND helps secure the Band Protocol's decentralized oracle network and earn rewards for supporting its infrastructure.

### Overview of the Band Protocol

Band Protocol is a cross-chain data oracle that aggregates and connects real-world data to blockchain applications. By providing reliable and scalable data feeds, Band Protocol enables decentralized applications (dApps) to operate efficiently and securely.

Staking BAND allows token holders to support the oracle network's operations while earning rewards for their contributions.

### Prerequisites

Before you start staking BAND, ensure you have the following:

* **Compatible Wallet**: A wallet that supports BAND staking, such as [Keplr Wallet](https://www.keplr.app/) or [Cosmostation](https://www.cosmostation.io/).
* **BAND Tokens**: Ensure you have BAND tokens in your wallet for staking and transaction fees.
* **Chorus One Validator**: Locate the official [Chorus One Validator Address](https://www.mintscan.io/band-protocol/validators).
* **Block Explorer**: Use the [Mintscan Band Explorer](https://www.mintscan.io/band-protocol) to verify transactions and validator details.
* **Staking Rewards Info**: Check [Staking Rewards](https://www.stakingrewards.com/earn/band-protocol) for the latest APY rates.

For more details, refer to the FAQs.

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

1. Download and install a compatible wallet:
   * [Keplr Wallet](https://www.keplr.app/)
   * [Cosmostation](https://www.cosmostation.io/)
2. Create a new wallet and securely store your recovery phrase.
3. Transfer BAND tokens to your wallet.

\[IMAGE: Wallet setup interface]

***

#### Step 2: Access the Staking Interface

1. Open your wallet and navigate to the **Staking** or **Delegation** section.
2. Select the option to **Stake** or **Delegate** your BAND tokens.

\[IMAGE: Staking interface in the wallet]

***

#### Step 3: Select the Chorus One Validator

1. Search for the **Chorus One Validator** using the validator address: [Chorus One Validator Address](https://www.mintscan.io/band-protocol/validators).
2. Click on the validator to select it.

> **Tip**: Use the [Mintscan Band Explorer](https://www.mintscan.io/band-protocol) to verify the validator details, such as uptime and commission rates.

\[IMAGE: Validator selection screen]

***

#### Step 4: Delegate Your BAND Tokens

1. Enter the amount of BAND you wish to stake.
2. Review the transaction details, ensuring the validator is set to Chorus One.
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

\[IMAGE: Delegation amount entry]

***

#### Step 5: Confirm Your Delegation

1. Once the transaction is confirmed, your BAND tokens will be staked, and you will start earning rewards.
2. Check the **Staking Dashboard** in your wallet or use the [Mintscan Band Explorer](https://www.mintscan.io/band-protocol) to monitor your staked balance and rewards.

\[IMAGE: Staking confirmation screen]

***

### Managing Your Staked BAND

* **Unstaking**: To unstake your BAND, navigate to the staking interface, select your staked balance, and follow the steps to undelegate. Remember, there is a 21-day unbonding period.
* **Rewards**: Rewards are auto-compounded or claimable, depending on your wallet settings.

***

### FAQs

#### 1. What are the staking rewards for BAND?

Staking rewards depend on network parameters and validator performance. Check [Staking Rewards](https://www.stakingrewards.com/earn/band-protocol) for the latest APY rates.

#### 2. Is there a lock-up period for BAND staking?

Yes, there is a 21-day unbonding period when unstaking BAND tokens.

#### 3. How do I contact support?

For questions or assistance, reach out to [Chorus One Support](https://chorus.one/contact).
