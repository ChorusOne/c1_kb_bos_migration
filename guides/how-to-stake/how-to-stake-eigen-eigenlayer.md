# How to stake EIGEN (EigenLayer)

## How to Stake EigenLayer (EIGEN)

Welcome to the step-by-step guide on how to stake EIGEN on EigenLayer using Chorus One. Staking EIGEN helps secure the EigenLayer protocol while allowing you to earn rewards. You can stake directly via the Eigen app or leverage the OPUS Pool managed by Chorus One for a streamlined staking experience.

### Overview of EigenLayer

EigenLayer is a protocol that enables restaking, allowing users to extend the utility of their staked tokens across multiple services without compromising security. By participating in EigenLayer, users can enhance decentralized security while maximizing staking yields.

As a modular network, EigenLayer empowers developers with customizable economic security while incentivizing users to restake their existing tokens. Whether you're restaking ETH or participating with native EIGEN tokens, the protocol ensures flexibility and rewards for contributors.

### Prerequisites

Before you start staking EIGEN, ensure you have the following:

* **Compatible Wallet**: A wallet that supports EigenLayer interactions, such as MetaMask.
* **EIGEN or Restakable Tokens**: Tokens ready for staking or restaking on EigenLayer.
* **Gas Fees**: Ensure you have enough ETH for transaction fees.
* **Chorus One OPUS Pool Details**: For staking through the OPUS Pool, review the pool details and requirements.

For more details, refer to the FAQs.

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### Option 1: Stake via the Eigen App

**Step 1: Connect Your Wallet**

1. Open the [Eigen app](https://eigenlayer.io).
2. Connect your wallet (e.g., MetaMask) to the platform.
3. Ensure you have EIGEN tokens in your wallet or restakable tokens.

\[IMAGE: Eigen app wallet connection interface]

**Step 2: Choose a Staking Option**

1. Navigate to the **Staking** section of the Eigen app.
2. Select the **Restaking** or **Native Staking** option, depending on the tokens you wish to stake.

\[IMAGE: Staking options in the Eigen app]

**Step 3: Delegate or Restake**

1. Enter the amount of tokens to stake or restake.
2. Select the validator of your choice. If applicable, choose **Chorus One** for trusted validation services.
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

\[IMAGE: Token delegation interface]

***

#### Option 2: Stake via the OPUS Pool with Chorus One

**Step 1: Access the OPUS Pool**

1. Visit the [Chorus One OPUS Pool page](https://chorus.one).
2. Review the pool details, including minimum staking requirements and expected returns.

\[IMAGE: OPUS Pool interface]

**Step 2: Deposit Tokens into the Pool**

1. Click **Deposit** and connect your wallet to the interface.
2. Enter the amount of EIGEN or restakable tokens to deposit into the OPUS Pool.
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

\[IMAGE: Token deposit interface]

**Step 3: Monitor Your Stake**

1. Access the OPUS Pool dashboard to view your staked balance and accrued rewards.
2. Withdraw or manage your stake directly through the interface.

\[IMAGE: OPUS Pool dashboard]

***

### Managing Your Staked Tokens

* **Unstaking**: For the Eigen app, navigate to the **Unstake** section and follow the steps. For OPUS Pool, use the withdrawal option in the dashboard.
* **Rewards**: Rewards are auto-compounded or claimable, depending on your chosen method.
