# ❌ \[OLD] How to stake NYM (Nym)

## How to Stake NYM (Nym)

Welcome to the step-by-step guide on how to stake NYM tokens on the Nym network using Chorus One. Staking NYM helps secure the Nym network while providing rewards for supporting its privacy-focused infrastructure.

### Overview of the Nym Network

Nym is a privacy infrastructure protocol designed to prevent data surveillance and metadata leaks. By leveraging mixnets and anonymous credentials, Nym offers developers the tools to build privacy-preserving applications. Its mission is to create a decentralized, private-by-default internet for users worldwide.

Staking NYM helps power the Nym mixnet, ensuring its robustness and efficiency while earning rewards for contributing to the network's decentralization.

### Prerequisites

Before you start staking NYM, ensure you have the following:

* **Compatible Wallet**: A wallet that supports NYM tokens, such as [Nym Wallet](https://nymtech.net/download/) or [Keplr Wallet](https://www.keplr.app/).
* **NYM Tokens**: Ensure you have NYM tokens in your wallet, along with a small amount for transaction fees.
* **Chorus One Validator**: Locate the official Chorus One validator address in the staking interface.
* **Block Explorer**: Use the [Nym Explorer](https://explorer.nymtech.net/) to verify transactions and validator information.

For more details, refer to the FAQs.

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

1. Download and install a compatible wallet:
   * [Nym Wallet](https://nymtech.net/download/)
   * [Keplr Wallet](https://www.keplr.app/)
2. Create a new wallet and securely store your recovery phrase.
3. Transfer NYM tokens to your wallet.

\[IMAGE: Wallet setup interface]

***

#### Step 2: Access the Staking Interface

1. Open your wallet and navigate to the **Staking** or **Delegation** section.
2. Select the option to **Stake** or **Delegate** your NYM tokens.

\[IMAGE: Staking interface in the wallet]

***

#### Step 3: Select the Chorus One Validator

1. Search for the **Chorus One Validator** using the validator address: [Chorus One Validator Address](https://nymtech.net)
2. Click on the validator to select it.

> **Tip**: Use the [Nym Explorer](https://explorer.nymtech.net/) to verify the Chorus One validator details, such as uptime and commission rates.

\[IMAGE: Validator selection screen]

***

#### Step 4: Delegate Your NYM Tokens

1. Enter the amount of NYM you wish to stake.
2. Review the transaction details, ensuring the validator is set to Chorus One.
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

\[IMAGE: Delegation amount entry]

***

#### Step 5: Confirm Your Delegation

1. Once the transaction is confirmed, your NYM tokens will be staked, and you will start earning rewards.
2. Check the **Staking Dashboard** in your wallet or use the [Nym Explorer](https://explorer.nymtech.net/) to monitor your staked balance and rewards.

\[IMAGE: Staking confirmation screen]

***

### Managing Your Staked NYM

* **Unstaking**: To unstake your NYM, navigate to the staking interface, select your staked balance, and follow the steps to undelegate.
* **Rewards**: Rewards are auto-compounded or claimable, depending on the Nym network rules.

### FAQs

#### 1. What are the staking rewards for NYM?

Staking rewards vary based on network parameters, validator performance, and the total staked amount.

#### 2. Is there a lock-up period for NYM staking?

Yes, there is typically a 21-day unbonding period. Check the current network rules for details.

#### 3. How do I contact support?

For questions or assistance, reach out to [Chorus One Support](https://chorus.one/contact).
