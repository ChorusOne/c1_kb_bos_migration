# How to stake INJ (Injective)

## Overview <a href="#h_01hc8bcz8fnx3qxz8bd04rk2s3" id="h_01hc8bcz8fnx3qxz8bd04rk2s3"></a>

The Injective Protocol (INJ) is a Layer 1 blockchain network and smart contract platform that's designed for trading applications and decentralized finance (DeFi) platforms.&#x20;

* Layer 1 blockchains, like Injective, operate their own independent networks, rather than being built on top of another blockchain like Layer 2 solutions.

Injective Protocol is highly interoperable with other networks and features its own fully decentralized ERC-20 token bridge, supporting native Ethereum-based assets within its ecosystem.&#x20;

The central platform of Injective Protocol is the Injective Hub. By connecting to the Injective Hub, Defi users can take advantage of its many features including token swaps, staking rewards, and governance proposals.

{% hint style="info" %}
Injective is built with the Cosmos SDK and utilizes a Tendermint proof-of-stake consensus mechanism for secure transactions with instant finality, sporting an order book module on-chain and is highly interoperable with prominent layer 1s, including Ethereum and Cosmos.
{% endhint %}

## Keplr <a href="#h_01hc83zgc05n1kf73fq3ejrcjc" id="h_01hc83zgc05n1kf73fq3ejrcjc"></a>

### 1. Installing the Keplr Wallet Extension <a href="#h_01hc83zgc05n1kf73fq3ejrcjc" id="h_01hc83zgc05n1kf73fq3ejrcjc"></a>

In case you don't have the Keplr extension installed in your browser visit [https://www.keplr.app/](https://www.keplr.app/) and click on Install extension.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (57).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="https://uploads-ssl.webflow.com/63fdf8c863bcf00d7ffdff91/6419606a2f4ea84e4f721641_image1.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click on **Install Keplr for Chrome** if you are using a Chrome browser or **Brave** if you are using the Brave browser and follow the installation instructions.

### 2. Create/Important a Keplr Account <a href="#h_01hc844cgfe24z98sh3qf5zf24" id="h_01hc844cgfe24z98sh3qf5zf24"></a>

Click on the extension in the Chrome/Brave toolbar and the following page will open up.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-20 at 5.05.09 PM.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
In case you do not have an existing Keplr account you can click '**Create a new wallet**'.&#x20;

If you already have a wallet to use, you can select '**Import an existing wallet**' or you can connect with a compatible hardware wallet, such as Ledger.\
\
Alternatively, Keplr now offers the ability to associate your wallet with your Google account, however, please note that this is a less secure way of establishing your wallet.
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-20 at 5.07.19 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>It is recommended to create a new wallet that you control the mnemonic phrase of, or use a hardware wallet.</p></figcaption></figure>

**If you choose to create a new wallet you will be shown 12 words as your mnemonic seed.**&#x20;

* You can select the 24 words option for a more secure mnemonic.&#x20;

_Back it up securely (read the warning below)_

{% hint style="warning" %}
_**Please be sure to back up your mnemonic seed securely. Never share this seed phrase with anyone, as they will have access to your funds.**_&#x20;

* Lost mnemonic seed can't be recovered.
* Anyone with your mnemonic seed can take your assets.
{% endhint %}

Enter an account name and a passphrase to unlock your wallet. You will be asked for the mnemonic again.&#x20;

Enter the 12 or 24 words in order and case sensitive (all lower case).&#x20;

This is to make sure you remember the mnemonic.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-20 at 5.18.45 PM.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption><p>Please write this down carefully and never share it with anyone.</p></figcaption></figure>

After verifying your 12 or 24 word phrase, you will be prompted to select any other Cosmos Hub networks you'd like to add to your wallet.&#x20;

* _**In this case, we will be adding INJ, so please be sure to select that from the list or use the search bar to find it.**_

{% hint style="info" %}
No need to add any other networks if you don't plan on using them yet. You can always select more networks later. &#x20;

However, it is advisable to have '**Cosmos Hub**' selected when creating your new wallet.

* Remember, INJ can be found via the search bar, highlighted below.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-20 at 5.21.13 PM (1).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once you selected the relevant networks, click '**Save**' and you'll be all set to go.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-20 at 5.23.52 PM.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption><p>Congratulations! Your wallet is set up and ready to go!</p></figcaption></figure>

### 3. Log in to Your Keplr Account <a href="#h_01hc8b1t0xqz8tdapbp8g98nkt" id="h_01hc8b1t0xqz8tdapbp8g98nkt"></a>

Regardless of whether you already have an account or if you just created it, you may now click on the extension to view your address or visit [https://wallet.keplr.app/?tab=overview](https://wallet.keplr.app/?tab=overview) to see your full Keplr dashboard.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-20 at 5.25.10 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>You can see on the left hand side of the menu what networks you have added to your wallet. (ATOM and OSMO shown here)</p></figcaption></figure>

If you don't already have INJ in your account, you can fund your wallet with some tokens.&#x20;

You may use an exchange to transfer the INJ tokens to your address or get it from someone who already holds them.

### 4. Staking via Keplr

If you want to stake from the Keplr browser extension wallet, you can either navigate to the Keplr dashboard (shown below) or scroll down on the bworser wallet screen and select INJ.&#x20;

From there, you will be prompted to stake.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-20 at 5.28.41 PM.png" alt="" width="356"><figcaption><p>How to access the dashboard from the wallet browser extension.</p></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-07-12 at 2.54.45 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Or stake from within the wallet by scrolling down and selecting INJ.</p></figcaption></figure>

Once you are on the Keplr dashboard, to stake click on the '**Stake**' tab in the left hand side of the dashboard.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-07-12 at 2.57.10 PM.png" alt="" width="179"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once you click '**Stake**', you will see three steps highlighted in the pink box in the screenshot below.&#x20;

1. **Select Chain**
2. **Select Validator**
3. **Stake**

Scroll or through the list or search for the chain you want (in this case **INJ**).

* Then, you can once again either search or scroll through the list to find **Chorus One.**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-06-14 at 1.08.13 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Here's how the networks selection list will look.</p></figcaption></figure>

Once you've clicked on the Chorus One validator, select how much INJ you wish to stake, then click the '**Stake**' button at the bottom of the screen.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Please be sure to leave a small amount of INJ unstakd in your wallet to cover gas fees in the future to claim rewards or unstake.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

Clicking on Stake will take you to Keplr wallet for approval. Approve the transaction and you will be able to see your stake.

Once your transaction is approved you will be able to see your INJ being staked.&#x20;

* **Congratulations you have successfully staked your INJ!**

{% hint style="info" %}
Please note that there is a 21-day unbonding process (also known as unstaking) for INJ tokens during which the staked INJ does not earn rewards and cannot be transferred, exchanged, or spent.
{% endhint %}

### 5. Claiming Rewards in Keplr <a href="#h_01hc8b1t0xyhfh6z6pc14htt2d" id="h_01hc8b1t0xyhfh6z6pc14htt2d"></a>

After some time you will see rewards getting accumulated in your account.&#x20;

Here's an example of how this might look:&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-07-12 at 2.26.40 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>You can also go to your Keplr Dashboard to claim rewards too and see a greater detail breakdown. </p></figcaption></figure>

You can simply go to the Keplr extension to claim them by selecting '**Claim**' and approving the transaction.&#x20;

{% hint style="success" %}
And there you go! You've successfully claimed your rewards after approving and submitting the transaction to the network!&#x20;
{% endhint %}

## Staking via Injective Hub

### 1. Access the Hub <a href="#h_01hc8bf5m5djgzg0vf3bee2esw" id="h_01hc8bf5m5djgzg0vf3bee2esw"></a>

If you'd like to stake via the Injective Hub. Go to [https://hub.injective.network/](https://hub.injective.network/) and click on '**Connect**' on the top-right corner to connect with any wallet currently integrated with Injective

* These include: [Keplr](https://www.keplr.app/), [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/), [Ledger](https://www.ledger.com/), [Cosmostation](https://www.cosmostation.io/), [Leap](https://www.leapwallet.io/), [Torus](https://blog.injective.com/injective-integrates-torus-wallet-to-enable-social-logins-for-mainstream-users/), or [Trezor](https://blog.injective.com/injective-becomes-the-first-cosmos-ecosystem-blockchain-to-integrate-trezor-to-further-hardware-wallet-support/) wallets to name a few.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-07-12 at 3.10.09 PM.png" alt="" width="425"><figcaption><p>Supported wallet list</p></figcaption></figure>

### 2. Explore the Dashboard <a href="#h_01hc8bf5m5dm7fmp65d1v1baj0" id="h_01hc8bf5m5dm7fmp65d1v1baj0"></a>

Once you get connected you can view your dashboard.&#x20;

On the top right, you can view and copy your INJ address the Disconnect option is also found here.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-07-12 at 2.47.40 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Injective Hub Dashboard</p></figcaption></figure>

### 3. Delegate your INJ Tokens <a href="#h_01hc8bf5m54cy9v9p37cvfn6pw" id="h_01hc8bf5m54cy9v9p37cvfn6pw"></a>

On the dashboard, you can click on Delegate Now to view validators and delegate your INJ tokens to them.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-07-12 at 3.15.03 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

When you click the button, you are shown a dashboard of your staking stats and earnings, claimable rewards, and INJ availble in your wallet.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-07-12 at 3.15.57 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Search for '**Chorus One**' in the search box, or use [this link to go to the Chorus One validator](https://hub.injective.network/validators/injvaloper14yeq3lkajldaggj28hmq8xng9xux7x5g46hezv/) and click on '**Delegate Now**'.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-07-12 at 3.18.53 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

A popup window will appear, and you can specify the amount of INJ you wish to stake.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-07-12 at 3.21.45 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="warning" %}
Please be sure to leave a small amount of INJ unstakd in your wallet to cover gas fees in the future to claim rewards or unstake.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

Click on '**Delegate**' and you will be asked to sign a transaction. Doing so will finalize your staking transaction.

{% hint style="success" %}
Congratulations! You've successfully delegated your INJ tokens. You can head back to the '**Wallet**' page to view your delegations.
{% endhint %}

### 4. Claiming Rewards and Unbonding <a href="#h_01hc8bf5m5stwbbch76sxf3vd1" id="h_01hc8bf5m5stwbbch76sxf3vd1"></a>

You claim your rewards, distributed continuously every block, on the '**Wallet**' page.&#x20;

When you unbond your tokens, you will stop receiving rewards, and your original INJ balance will be returned to you after the unbonding period, which is 21 days.&#x20;

* Note: While 21 days is the standard, it is possible for this unbonding period can be changed via community-based governance.
