# How to stake CELO (Celo Network)

## How to Stake CELO

Welcome to the step-by-step guide on how to stake CELO on the Celo network using Chorus One. Staking CELO helps secure the Celo blockchain and earn rewards for supporting its mobile-first infrastructure.

### Overview of the Celo Network

Celo is a Layer 1 blockchain designed to bring financial tools to anyone with a smartphone, making Web3 technology accessible to global users. With a focus on mobile-friendly dApps, stablecoins, and environmental sustainability, Celo empowers users to participate in the decentralized economy.

By staking CELO, you contribute to securing the network and promoting financial inclusion worldwide.

### Prerequisites

Before you start staking CELO, ensure you have the following:

* **Compatible Wallet**: A wallet that supports Celo staking, such as [Valora Wallet](https://valoraapp.com/) or [Celo Wallet](https://celowallet.app/).
* **CELO Tokens**: Ensure you have CELO tokens in your wallet for staking and transaction fees.
* **Chorus One Validator**: Locate the official [Chorus One Validator Address](https://explorer.celo.org/validators).
* **Block Explorer**: Use the [Celo Explorer](https://explorer.celo.org/) to verify transactions and validator details.
* **Staking Rewards Info**: Check [Staking Rewards](https://www.stakingrewards.com/earn/celo) for the latest APY rates.

For more details, refer to the FAQs.

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

1. Download and install a compatible wallet:
   * [Valora Wallet](https://valoraapp.com/)
   * [Celo Wallet](https://celowallet.app/)
2. Create a new wallet and securely store your recovery phrase.
3. Transfer CELO tokens to your wallet.

\[IMAGE: Wallet setup interface]

***

#### Step 2: Access the Staking Interface

1. Open your wallet and navigate to the **Staking** or **Governance** section.
2. Select the option to **Vote for Validators** or **Stake CELO**.

\[IMAGE: Staking interface in the wallet]

***

#### Step 3: Select the Chorus One Validator

1. Search for the **Chorus One Validator** using the validator address: [Chorus One Validator Address](https://explorer.celo.org/validators).
2. Click on the validator to select it.

> **Tip**: Use the [Celo Explorer](https://explorer.celo.org/) to verify the validator's details, such as uptime and commission rates.

\[IMAGE: Validator selection screen]

***

#### Step 4: Delegate Your CELO Tokens

1. Enter the amount of CELO you wish to stake.
2. Review the transaction details, ensuring the validator is set to Chorus One.
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

\[IMAGE: Delegation amount entry]

***

#### Step 5: Confirm Your Delegation

1. Once the transaction is confirmed, your CELO tokens will be staked, and you will start earning rewards.
2. Check the **Staking Dashboard** in your wallet or use the [Celo Explorer](https://explorer.celo.org/) to monitor your staked balance and rewards.

\[IMAGE: Staking confirmation screen]

***

### Managing Your Staked CELO

* **Unstaking**: To unstake your CELO, navigate to the staking interface, select your staked balance, and follow the steps to undelegate. Remember, there is a 3-day unbonding period.
* **Rewards**: Rewards are auto-compounded or claimable, depending on your wallet settings.

***

### FAQs

#### 1. What are the staking rewards for CELO?

Staking rewards depend on network parameters and validator performance. Check [Staking Rewards](https://www.stakingrewards.com/earn/celo) for the latest APY rates.

#### 2. Is there a lock-up period for CELO staking?

Yes, there is a 3-day unbonding period when unstaking CELO.

#### 3. How do I contact support?

For questions or assistance, reach out to [Chorus One Support](https://chorus.one/contact).
