## About Regen Network

**Regen Network (REGEN)** is a blockchain network focused on ecological sustainability, enabling projects and stakeholders to create, verify, and trade ecosystem services like carbon credits.&#x20;

Built on the Cosmos SDK, it offers a decentralized framework for measuring and valuing environmental impacts, ensuring transparency and accountability in ecological restoration initiatives. By leveraging smart contracts, Regen Network connects farmers, land stewards, and investors to incentivize regenerative practices, rewarding those who actively contribute to planetary health.

* The network’s native token, REGEN, facilitates governance and staking while powering transactions on the platform.&#x20;
* [Regen Marketplace](https://app.regen.network/), one of its flagship applications, allows users to mint, buy, and sell verified ecological assets, such as carbon sequestration credits.&#x20;

With its focus on climate action and a thriving community, Regen Network positions itself as a leader in utilizing blockchain technology for ecological transformation.

If you already have a wallet installed, feel free to skip ahead to [How to Stake](how-to-stake-regen-regen-network.md#h_01hc8b1t0x326w2f4nzezyfjz6).

### 2. Create/Import an Account <a href="#h_01hc8b1t0x9e4fj4j73357dgbw" id="h_01hc8b1t0x9e4fj4j73357dgbw"></a>

Click on the extension in the Chrome/Brave toolbar and the following page will open up.

* It is recommended to pin it to your browser toolbar by clicking on the jigsaw piece 🧩 icon and then the pin :pushpin: icon.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-20 at 5.05.09 PM.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption><p>Select to either create a new wallet, import an existing wallet, or connect with a hardware wallet.</p></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
In case you do not have an existing Keplr account you can click 'Create a new wallet'.&#x20;

If you already have a wallet to use, you can select 'Import an existing wallet' or you can connect with a compatible hardware wallet, such as a Ledger device.

* Alternatively, Keplr now offers the ability to associate your wallet with your Google account, however, this is a **less secure** way of establishing your wallet and is not recommended.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-20 at 5.07.19 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Here you can choose between creating, importing, or associating your wallet with your Google account. </p></figcaption></figure>

**If you choose to create a new wallet you will be shown 12 words as your mnemonic seed.**&#x20;

* **Optional:** You can select the 24 words option for a more secure mnemonic.&#x20;

{% hint style="danger" %}
**Please be sure to back up your mnemonic seed securely.**&#x20;

* It is recommended to store it physically; never in a digital format or as a screenshot.

**Never share this seed phrase with anyone, as they will have access to your funds.**&#x20;

* A lost mnemonic seed phrase cannot be recovered.
* Anyone with your mnemonic seed phrase can take control of your assets.
{% endhint %}

Next, enter an account name and a passphrase to lock and unlock your wallet. You will be asked for the mnemonic again.&#x20;

* Enter the 12 or 24 words in order and case sensitive (all lower case).&#x20;
* This is to make sure you remember the mnemonic and confirm that you wrote it down correctly.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-20 at 5.18.45 PM.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption><p>Example of the screen that will show your recovery phrase.</p></figcaption></figure>

After verifying your 12 or 24 word phrase, you will be prompted to select any other Cosmos Hub networks you'd like to add to your wallet.&#x20;

* **In this case, we will be adding Regen Network (REGEN), so please be sure to select that from the list or use the search bar to find it.**

{% hint style="info" %}
No need to add any other networks if you don't plan on using them yet. You can always select more networks later. &#x20;

However, it is advisable to have '**Cosmos Hub**' selected when creating your new wallet.
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-20 at 5.21.13 PM (1).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption><p>Be sure to search for REGEN in the list in addition to Cosmos Hub.</p></figcaption></figure>

Once you selected the relevant networks you want to use, click 'Save' and you'll be all set to go.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-20 at 5.23.52 PM.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption><p>All set!! Your Keplr wallet is good to go!</p></figcaption></figure>

### 4. Stake your REGEN <a href="#h_01hc8b1t0x326w2f4nzezyfjz6" id="h_01hc8b1t0x326w2f4nzezyfjz6"></a>

If you don't already have some REGEN in your wallet, you can fund it with some tokens. You may use an exchange to transfer the tokens to your address or get them from a trusted third party that already holds some.

If you want to stake from the browser extension wallet, you can either navigate to the [Keplr dashboard](https://wallet.keplr.app/) (shown below) or scroll down on the wallet screen and select REGEN.&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
Alternatively, you can connect Keplr to the Mintscan explorer and stake to the Chorus One validator at this address: [regenvaloper15urq2dtp9qce4fyc85m6upwm9xul3049l7gjdc](https://www.mintscan.io/regen/validators/regenvaloper15urq2dtp9qce4fyc85m6upwm9xul3049l7gjdc)

Or you can navigate to thenChorus One staking page and click on 'Stake Now'

* [https://chorus.one/crypto-staking-networks/regen](https://chorus.one/crypto-staking-networks/regen)
{% endhint %}

Next, you will be prompted to stake.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-05-20 at 5.28.41 PM.png" alt="" width="356"><figcaption><p>How to access the dashboard from the wallet browser extension</p></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-11-15 at 2.44.04 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Scroll down to find REGEN or use the search bar.</p></figcaption></figure>

If you click on REGEN directly from your wallet extension, you will see the option to stake. Click on that to be taken to the REGEN page of your dashboard.&#x20;

* Alternatively, you can access your entire Keplr staking dashboard [here](https://wallet.keplr.app/?tab=staking).&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-11-15 at 2.46.20 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Example of the REGEN dashboard in Keplr.</p></figcaption></figure>

Alternatively, if you are already on the [Keplr dashboard](https://wallet.keplr.app/), to stake click on the 'Stake' tab on the left hand side of the dashboard.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-10-02 at 5.07.29 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Keplr dashboard example</p></figcaption></figure>

Once there, you will see three steps highlighted in the pink box in the screenshot below.&#x20;

1. **Select Chain**
2. **Select Validator**
3. **Stake**

Scroll or through the list or search for the chain you want, in this case **REGEN**.

* Then, repeat the same steps to find the Chorus One validator.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-06-14 at 1.08.13 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Example of using the Keplr wallet screen to choose your chain and validator. </p></figcaption></figure>

Once you've chosen the Chorus One validator, select how many REGEN tokens you wish to stake, then click the 'Stake' button at the bottom of the screen.

Clicking on Stake will take you to Keplr wallet for approval. Approve the transaction and you will be able to see your stake.

* **Note:** If you are using a Ledger hardware wallet, you will need to have the wallet connected, unlocked, and approve the transaction there.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-11-15 at 2.48.31 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Example of the REGEN staking screen with Chorus One selected.</p></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
Please note that there is a 21 day unbonding process (also known as unstaking) for REGEN.

During this period your stake no longer earns rewards and cannot be transferred, exchanged, or spent.

However, you can cancel the unstaking process if you wish without penalty.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

### 6. Unstaking your REGEN

If you wish to unstake your REGEN tokens, you can do so from the same interface in Keplr that you used to stake.&#x20;

* Either go to the [Keplr dashboard](https://wallet.keplr.app/) or manage your asset directly from the browser extension window.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-11-15 at 2.49.36 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Example of managing your REGEN stake in the wallet extension.</p></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-11-15 at 2.50.23 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Example of the Keplr dashboard where you can see your active stake and selected validators. </p></figcaption></figure>

Simply click on the validator you wish to unstake from and you will be prompted with the following screen.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-11-15 at 2.51.17 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Example of the REGEN staking &#x26; unstaking screen.</p></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="warning" %}
Please note that REGEN undergoes a 21 day unbonding period when unstaking.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-11-15 at 2.51.45 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Example of the 21 day unstaking warning before finalizing the transaction.</p></figcaption></figure>

To proceed, click on 'Unstake' and follow the prompts to select the amount of REGEN you wish to unstake.

Then confirm and sign the transaction in your wallet.&#x20;

{% hint style="success" %}
And that's it! Your REGEN tokens will begin unbonding which you can track from your [Keplr dashboard](https://wallet.keplr.app/) under the Staking tab.

You can view and manage all ongoing unstaking transactions (undelegations) from your Keplr dashboard and cancel them if you change your mind.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-11-15 at 2.53.35 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Example above showing a REGEN unstaking transaction in progress. </p></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="success" %}
After the unbonding period is complete you will be able to transact with your unstaked REGEN tokens again!
{% endhint %}
