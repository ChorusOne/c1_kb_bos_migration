## About SKALE <a href="#h_01hc89ph1zr1vjrbe2z111ww9v" id="h_01hc89ph1zr1vjrbe2z111ww9v"></a>

The SKALE Network is a decentralized, Ethereum-native platform designed to improve the scalability and performance of decentralized applications (dApps). It enables developers to deploy their own high-throughput, low-latency blockchains, known as "elastic sidechains," which operate in tandem with Ethereum, reducing congestion and gas fees on the mainnet.&#x20;

These sidechains are fully customizable, allowing dApps to scale efficiently while benefiting from Ethereum's security and decentralization.&#x20;

SKALE uses its native token, SKL, for network operations such as staking, governance, and securing the network through validator nodes, while transaction fees on the network are paid in ETH.

## How to Stake <a href="#h_01hc89ph1zr1vjrbe2z111ww9v" id="h_01hc89ph1zr1vjrbe2z111ww9v"></a>

Staking your SKL can be done via the [SKALE Portal](https://portal.skale.space/) and [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/download/), which involves four main steps:

1. Create a MetaMask wallet if you don't have one already.
2. Connect your MetaMask wallet to: [https://portal.skale.space/](https://portal.skale.space/)
3. Select a validator to stake with, such as Chorus One: [https://portal.skale.space/staking/new/0/19](https://portal.skale.space/staking/new/0/19)
4. Manage you SKL stake

{% hint style="info" %}
Before you get started, please make sure to have the MetaMask Browser Extension installed and to have enough ETH in your wallet to cover transaction fees.

* If you are using a Ledger device, make sure it is updated to the latest firmware and ensure you have the latest version of the Ethereum app installed.
* Please note that you will need to stake more than 100 SKL.
{% endhint %}

### MetaMask Setup Guide

For a full walkthrough on setting up MetaMask, please refer to their documentation found here:

* [Getting started with MetaMask](https://support.metamask.io/getting-started/getting-started-with-metamask/)

{% hint style="danger" %}

## 3. Select a Validator

From the left hand navigation bar, you can either go to '**Staking**' to see your current SKL balances and active stakes (if applicable), or you can navigate to '**Validators**' and select a validator to stake with.&#x20;

* The Chorus One validator can be found here: [https://portal.skale.space/staking/new/0/19](https://portal.skale.space/staking/new/0/19)

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-21 at 4.33.57 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Example of the 'Staking' tab.</p></figcaption></figure>

If you navigated to the '**Staking**' tab, you will see a screen similar to the screenshot above.&#x20;

In case you do not have any SKL tokens in your wallet, please make sure to properly fund it before you continue.&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
**Please note:** SKL transactions will require ETH to send, so be sure to keep some ETH in your wallet to pay for gas fees.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

When you are ready to stake, click on the green button '**Stake SKL**' to be taken to the '**Validators**' tab.&#x20;

From here, you will see a list of the active validators. Scroll through to find Chorus One and click on it.

A minimum of 100 SKL is required to stake. Staked funds are locked and illiquid for the entirety of the staking period.&#x20;

* The delegation time is 2 months and auto-renew will be turned on by default.&#x20;

### Epochs and Delegation Time

{% hint style="info" %}
Once you have initiated staking, your SKL stake will become active in the subsequent epoch and will remain locked for the entire staking period.&#x20;

* The validator you chose to stake with must also first accept your delegation.
{% endhint %}

To help explain, epochs are units of time within the SKALE Network. They equal a one month calendar period of network activity.&#x20;

* They always start on the 1st of each calendar month.&#x20;
* Rewards for validators and delegators are distributed at the end of each epoch and you are able to claim them within several days after the end of the epoch.&#x20;
* Please note that claiming your rewards incurs a transaction fee, so make sure to have enough ETH in your wallet to cover the fees.&#x20;
* Your rewards do not expire and you can claim them whenever you please.&#x20;

> This means that if you began staking your SKL on August 21st, your delegation would be accepted by the validator and go live in the next epoch, starting on September 1st.&#x20;

## 5. Staking Auto-Renewal & Rewards <a href="#h_01hc89ph20ca6dvj4bx8qfqc3b" id="h_01hc89ph20ca6dvj4bx8qfqc3b"></a>

The default setting of SKL staking is set to “Auto-renew”.&#x20;

This means that your staking period is automatically renewed for the same period as you initially selected (2 calendar months starting on the 1st).&#x20;

* Hence, your tokens are automatically re-delegated to your chosen validator at the end of your delegation period.&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
You can always choose to stop this by visiting the '**Staking**' tab and switching auto-renew to off.

* Please note that this can only be done once your stake is active.&#x20;
* This will also incur a transaction fee that requires ETH to complete.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Please make sure to stop the auto-renewal a few days before your staking period ends if you do not wish for it to auto-renew.**

* Turning off auto-renew cannot be reversed.&#x20;
* Please only turn off auto-renew if you want your SKL to become liquid after your staking period ends.‍
{% endhint %}

## 6. Claiming Rewards

To claim your rewards, visit the '**Staking**' tab and click '**Retrieve**' to claim your SKL rewards you have accrued during your staking period.&#x20;

* This will incur a transaction fee that requires ETH to complete.
* Approve the transaction in MetaMask, and in case you are using a Ledger device, sign the transaction via your hardware wallet.

{% hint style="success" %}
Rewards for validators and delegators are distributed at the end of each epoch and you are able to claim them within several days after the end of the epoch.&#x20;

* Your rewards do not expire and you can claim them whenever you please.&#x20;
{% endhint %}
