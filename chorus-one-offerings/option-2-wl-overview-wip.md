## Introducing Chorus One’s White Label Staking Solutions

We are excited to offer White Label (WL) staking solutions at Chorus One, a service designed to maximize your personal branding, security, and control by providing dedicated private validators exclusively for your organization.&#x20;

**Our White Label solutions bring numerous advantages, including:**

• Customized Branding: Showcase each validator as your own exclusive staking service, or choose to remain anonymous.

• Full Control: Set fees and participate in network governance independently.

• Enhanced Compliance: Operate with minimized risk and heightened security through exclusive, private validators.

• Privacy and Compliance Options: Manage your validators across specific geographical regions for optimized privacy and regulatory compliance.

***

### What is a White Label Validator?

White Label validators are dedicated, private validators that give clients unmatched control, such as complete customizability, whitelisting delegation addresses, setting commission fees, as well as the ability to participate directly in network governance and voting.

With White Label, clients have the flexibility to use a single validator or multiple dedicated validators across a variety of networks all while branding them as your own service or keeping them entirely private for a select audience.
