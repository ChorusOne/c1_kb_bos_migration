## Non-Custodial Wallet Safety

Non-custodial wallets offer full control over your crypto but require diligent security practices to ensure your funds stay safe.

It is of paramount importance to safeguard your seed phrase, use strong passwords, avoid online storage of seed phrases or private keys, and to consider utilizing a hardware wallet for large holdings.&#x20;

To learn more, please see [crypto-security-best-practices.md](../../guides/faqs/crypto-security-best-practices.md "mention") and [what-is-a-seed-phrase.md](../../guides/faqs/what-is-a-seed-phrase.md "mention")
